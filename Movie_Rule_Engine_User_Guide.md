# Movie Ticket Rule Engine - User Guide

## Overview

The Movie Ticket Rule Engine is an intelligent system within CardCompass that analyzes your movie ticket purchases and recommends the optimal combination of credit cards and booking platforms to maximize your savings.

## Features

### 🎯 Smart Optimization
- **Automatic Card Selection**: Analyzes all your cards to find the best deals
- **Platform Recommendations**: Suggests optimal booking platforms (BookMyShow, PVR, INOX, etc.)
- **Transaction Splitting**: Recommends how to split purchases across multiple cards for maximum savings
- **Efficiency Threshold**: Prevents wasteful use of high-value benefits on low-value tickets

### 💰 Benefit Types Supported
- **BOGO (Buy One Get One)**: Free tickets with purchase
- **Percentage Discounts**: Fixed percentage off ticket prices
- **Cashback Offers**: Money back on purchases
- **Milestone Rewards**: Free tickets after spending thresholds

### 📊 Weekly Milestone Caching
- Tracks your progress towards milestone rewards
- Updates weekly for optimal performance
- Considers your current spending patterns

## How to Use

### 1. Access the Movie Analyzer
- Open CardCompass app
- Go to **Smart Transaction Advisor**
- Select the **Movies** tab

### 2. Enter Your Details
- **Number of Tickets**: How many tickets you want to buy
- **Price per Ticket**: Cost of each ticket in ₹
- **Preferred Platform** (Optional): BookMyShow, PVR, INOX, etc.
- **Preferred Cinema** (Optional): Specific cinema chain preference

### 3. Get Recommendations
- Tap **"Find Best Deals"**
- The system analyzes all your cards and benefits
- You'll see up to 3 optimized recommendations

### 4. Review Your Strategy
- **Transaction Steps**: How to split your purchase
- **Savings Breakdown**: Exact amount you'll save
- **Final Cost**: Your total cost after all discounts

**Input:**
- 7 tickets at ₹280 each (Total: ₹1,960)

**Output:**
1. **Buy 2 tickets** using ICICI Sapphire on BookMyShow
   - BOGO offer saves ₹280
2. **Use milestone voucher** for 2 free tickets with Diners Club Black
   - Saves ₹500
3. **Buy remaining 3 tickets** using Axis Burgundy
   - 25% cashback saves ₹210

**Total Savings: ₹990 (50.6% discount)**

## Key Benefits

### ✅ Prevents Inefficient Usage
The system won't recommend using your ICICI Emerald BOGO (₹750 max) for ₹280 tickets when you have ICICI Sapphire BOGO (₹300 max) available.

### ✅ Platform Flexibility
When savings are equal across platforms, the system shows all options so you can choose based on convenience.

### ✅ Real-time Analysis
All recommendations are calculated in real-time based on:
- Current benefit configurations
- Your monthly usage limits
- Available milestone progress
- Card-specific restrictions

### ✅ Top 3 Display
Shows the 3 most efficient strategies, ranked by savings per ticket.

## Understanding Recommendations

### Benefit Types Explained

**BOGO (Buy One Get One)**
- Get free tickets with your purchase
- Usually limited to specific platforms
- May have maximum discount caps

**Percentage Discounts**
- Fixed percentage off your total
- Often have monthly or transaction limits
- May require minimum purchase amounts

**Cashback**
- Money credited back to your account
- Usually processed with your statement
- May have annual caps

**Milestone Rewards**
- Earned after reaching spending thresholds
- Free tickets or vouchers
- Tracked weekly for accuracy

### Efficiency Logic

The system uses efficiency thresholds to prevent wasteful benefit usage:

- **High-value benefits** (like ₹750 BOGO) are reserved for premium tickets
- **Medium-value benefits** (like ₹300 BOGO) work well for regular tickets
- **Cashback benefits** are suitable for any ticket price

This ensures you get maximum value from your premium card benefits.

## Troubleshooting

### No Recommendations Found?
- Check if you have cards with movie benefits
- Verify your cards are active in the app
- Ensure ticket price meets minimum transaction requirements

### Limited Savings?
- You may have reached monthly benefit limits
- Consider splitting purchase across multiple months
- Check for alternative cards or benefits

### Platform Restrictions?
- Some benefits only work on specific platforms
- Try selecting "Any Platform" for more options
- Check benefit terms for platform requirements

## Tips for Maximum Savings

1. **Plan Ahead**: Check your milestone progress before booking
2. **Split Strategically**: Use different cards for different ticket batches
3. **Weekend Bookings**: Some benefits only apply on weekends
4. **Group Bookings**: Larger purchases often trigger better benefits
5. **Platform Shop**: Compare recommendations across platforms
